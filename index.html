🏆 Math Competitions in North America

Explore famous mathematics competitions for students — from elementary to high school level.

🦘 International
Math Kangaroo

Grades 1–12 · March every year

The world's largest math competition with over 6 million participants in 90+ countries. Clever, fun problems designed to spark joy in math — not just test speed.

Grades 1–12 Multiple Choice 75 min 30 questions

mathkangaroo.org
🔵 USA · MAA
AMC 8

Grades ≤ 8 · January every year

Organized by the Mathematical Association of America — the entry point to the prestigious AMC/AIME/USAMO pathway. 25 questions, 40 minutes. No penalty for wrong answers!

Grade ≤ 8 25 questions 40 min No penalty

maa.org
📐 USA · National
MATHCOUNTS

Grades 6–8 · School → State → National

A national middle school program with four rounds: Sprint, Target, Team, and the exciting Countdown Round. Fast-paced, competitive, and a rite of passage for young mathematicians.

Grades 6–8 4 Rounds Team + Individual Nationals in May

mathcounts.org
🍁 Canada · Waterloo
Gauss Contest

Grades 7 & 8 · May every year

Run by the CEMC at the University of Waterloo — a beloved Canadian tradition named after Carl Friedrich Gauss. 25 multiple-choice questions in 60 minutes. Top scorers recognized nationally.

Grades 7–8 25 questions 60 min U of Waterloo

cemc.uwaterloo.ca
